## 2. Brand Positioning

### Positioning Statement
> For discerning diners in Casablanca who seek meaningful culinary experiences, Epictete is the contemporary Mediterranean restaurant that combines Stoic philosophy with farm-fresh cuisine, because we believe dining should be intentional, mindful, and exceptional.

### Key Differentiators
1. **Stoic Philosophy Concept** — Only restaurant with philosophical foundation
2. **Own Organic Farm** — True farm-to-table, not just marketing
3. **Wood-Fired Authenticity** — Traditional Neapolitan pizza oven
4. **Design Excellence** — Instagram-worthy Art Deco Mediterranean space
5. **Bouskoura Location** — Serves growing upscale suburban market

### Competitive Positioning Map

```
                    FORMAL/UPSCALE
                         ↑
                         |
    Traditional ←———— EPICTETE ————→ Contemporary
    Moroccan             |              Mediterranean
                         |
                         ↓
                    CASUAL/RELAXED
```

## 5. Content Strategy

### Content Pillars & Distribution

| Pillar | % of Content | Content Types |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **The Food** | 40% | Dish photos, videos, menu highlights |
| **The Experience** | 25% | Ambiance, table settings, guest moments |
| **The Philosophy** | 15% | Quotes, mindfulness, Stoic wisdom |
| **Behind the Scenes** | 15% | Kitchen, farm, staff, process |
| **Community** | 5% | Events, partnerships, features |

### Content Calendar Framework

**Monday:** Food focus (start week with appetite appeal)
**Tuesday:** Behind-the-scenes (mid-week engagement)
**Wednesday:** Philosophy quote or thought
**Thursday:** Weekend teaser / special highlight
**Friday:** Atmosphere / weekend vibes
**Saturday:** Live content / Stories focus
**Sunday:** Family / relaxed dining content

### Hero Content (Monthly)

- 1 professional photo shoot
- 2-3 high-production Reels
- 1 influencer collaboration
- 1 special menu/event content

## 7. Influencer Strategy

### Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Approach | Budget |
|------|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Nano** | 1K-10K | Gifted meals | Low |
| **Micro** | 10K-50K | Gifted + small fee | Medium |
| **Mid** | 50K-100K | Paid partnership | Higher |
| **Macro** | 100K+ | Strategic partnership | Premium |

### Target Influencer Categories
- Food bloggers (Morocco-focused)
- Lifestyle influencers (Casablanca)
- Wellness/mindfulness accounts
- Design/architecture enthusiasts
- Local celebrities/personalities

### Collaboration Types
1. **Hosted Dinners:** Invite for complimentary meal, encourage organic content
2. **Paid Posts:** Specific deliverables, messaging guidelines
3. **Events:** Influencer nights, exclusive previews
4. **Takeovers:** Stories takeover by chef or influencer
5. **Long-term Ambassadors:** Ongoing relationship with aligned creators
