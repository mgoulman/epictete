**Frequency:** 2-3 per week
**Duration:** 15-30 seconds
**Style:**

- Pizza oven flames

**Trending Audio:** Use when relevant and on-brand
**Original Audio:** Kitchen sounds, ambient restaurant noise

- **Slides:** 3-7 images

- **Middle:** Details, process, angles
- **Last:** CTA or branded element

## 6. Engagement Strategy

### Daily Engagement Tasks
| Task | Time | Duration |
|------|------|----------|
| Respond to comments | Morning | 15 min |
| Reply to DMs | Morning | 15 min |
| Engage with tagged content | Midday | 10 min |

### Engagement Guidelines
- **Response Time:** Within 2-4 hours during operating hours
- **Tone:** Warm, professional, grateful
- **Emojis:** Use sparingly (1-2 per response)
- **Personalization:** Use name when possible

### Community Building
- Repost UGC in Stories (with credit)
- Feature loyal customers
- Engage with food bloggers' content
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)

## 10. Crisis Management

### Negative Comment Protocol
1. **Acknowledge** within 4 hours
2. **Apologize** for their experience
3. **Move offline** - request DM or call
4. **Resolve** the issue
5. **Follow up** to ensure satisfaction

### Do Not
- Delete negative comments (unless abusive)
- Argue publicly
- Make excuses
- Ignore the issue

### Escalation
- Report to management for serious issues
- Document all interactions
- Learn from feedback
