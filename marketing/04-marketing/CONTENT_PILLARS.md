# Epictete - Content Pillars Guide

> **Last Updated:** January 2026  
> **Version:** 1.0

---

## Content Pillar Overview

Content pillars are the foundational themes that guide all marketing content. They ensure consistency, relevance, and strategic alignment across all channels.

---

## Pillar 1: THE FOOD (40% of content)

### Purpose
Showcase culinary excellence, drive appetite appeal, and demonstrate quality.

### Sub-Topics

| Sub-Topic | Content Examples |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Dish Spotlights** | Hero shots of signature dishes |
| **Menu Highlights** | New items, seasonal specials |
| **Close-up Details** | Textures, ingredients, garnishes |
| **Action Shots** | Pouring, plating, cooking |
| **Pizza Focus** | Wood-fired oven, pizza preparation |
| **Dessert Features** | Sweet endings, indulgence moments |
| **Ingredient Stories** | Farm produce, quality sourcing |

### Content Formats
- Static photo posts (hero shots)
- Carousel posts (multiple angles)
- Short video (plating, pouring)
- Reels (satisfying food content)
- Stories (daily specials)

### Sample Captions
- "Every slice tells a story of fire and patience 🔥"
- "Fresh from the farm, fire to plate"
- "When the cheese pulls just right..."
- "Our wood-fired oven doesn't compromise. Neither do we."
- "Handmade. Everyday. Because shortcuts aren't on our menu."

### Best Posting Times
- Lunch: 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM
- Dinner: 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM
- Weekend: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

## Pillar 2: THE EXPERIENCE (25% of content)

### Purpose
Sell the atmosphere, create desire to visit, showcase the complete dining experience.

### Sub-Topics

| Sub-Topic | Content Examples |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Ambiance** | Interior shots, lighting, mood |
| **Table Settings** | Branded elements, place settings |
| **Details** | Design elements, textures, decor |
| **Guest Moments** | Happy diners (with permission) |
| **Special Occasions** | Birthdays, celebrations |
| **Arrival/Welcome** | Host stand, entrance, first impressions |
| **Natural Light** | Daytime atmosphere |
| **Evening Glow** | Romantic dinner setting |

### Content Formats
- Wide interior shots
- Detail close-ups
- Lifestyle photography (people enjoying)
- Video walkthroughs
- Time-lapse (day to night)

### Sample Captions
- "Where every corner invites you to linger"
- "Set the scene for an evening worth remembering"
- "Natural light, great company, exceptional food"
- "Your table is ready ✨"
- "Some places are made for special moments. This is one."

### Styling Notes
- Always show branded elements
- Capture natural light when possible
- Include human elements for warmth
- Highlight texture contrasts

## Pillar 3: THE PHILOSOPHY (15% of content)

### Purpose
Differentiate through unique concept, add depth to brand, connect emotionally.

### Sub-Topics

| Sub-Topic | Content Examples |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Stoic Quotes** | Adapted wisdom from Epictetus |
| **Mindfulness** | Present-moment dining messages |
| **Intentional Living** | Choice, quality, meaning |
| **Brand Philosophy** | "Savoure chaque instant" messaging |
| **Slow Food** | Counter to rush culture |
| **Gratitude** | Appreciation themes |

### Content Formats
- Quote graphics
- Text overlay on imagery
- Carousel with storytelling
- Thoughtful captions
- Stories with reflection prompts

### Sample Content

**Quote Posts:**
- "Some things are within our power, while others are not. Tonight, choose what nourishes you." — Inspired by Epictetus
- "The examined meal is worth eating."
- "Savor. That's not just a suggestion. It's a practice."

**Mindfulness Posts:**
- "Put the phone down (after taking the photo, of course). Tonight, be here."
- "Fast food feeds the body. Mindful dining feeds the soul."
- "Choose your ingredients. Choose your company. Choose your moment."

### Timing
- Best for quieter days (Tuesday, Wednesday)
- Morning posts for reflection
- Sunday contemplation content

## Pillar 4: BEHIND THE SCENES (15% of content)

### Purpose
Build authenticity, humanize the brand, create connection, showcase craftsmanship.

### Sub-Topics

| Sub-Topic | Content Examples |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Kitchen Action** | Cooking, plating, fire |
| **Farm Content** | Growing, harvesting, fresh produce |
| **Staff Spotlights** | Team members, their stories |
| **Prep Work** | Morning preparation, fresh starts |
| **Pizza Oven** | Fire management, dough tossing |
| **Handmade Process** | Pasta making, sauce preparation |
| **Delivery Arrivals** | Fresh ingredients arriving |
| **Setup/Breakdown** | Restaurant opening/closing rituals |

### Content Formats
- Raw, authentic video
- Stories (perfect for BTS)
- Reels with personality
- Time-lapse of processes
- Staff takeovers

### Sample Captions
- "5:30 AM. The kitchen is quiet, but the day has begun."
- "Meet the hands behind your meal 👨‍🍳"
- "Our wood-fired oven at 400°C. That's where the magic happens 🔥"
- "From our farm this morning, to your plate tonight 🌱"
- "Handmade pasta: 3 ingredients, 30 minutes, infinite patience"

### Authenticity Guidelines
- Less polished is okay
- Show real moments
- Include sounds (sizzling, chopping)
- Feature different team members
- Be genuine, not staged

## Pillar 5: COMMUNITY (5% of content)

### Purpose
Build local connections, show social proof, engage with community.

### Sub-Topics

| Sub-Topic | Content Examples |
|-----------|------------------|
| **User-Generated Content** | Reposting guest photos |
| **Reviews/Testimonials** | Sharing positive feedback |
| **Local Partnerships** | Collaborations, suppliers |
| **Events** | Special nights, hosted events |
| **Milestones** | Anniversaries, achievements |
| **Seasonal Celebrations** | Holidays, local events |
| **Thank You Posts** | Gratitude to community |

### Content Formats
- Reshared stories
- Review screenshots
- Partnership announcements
- Event recaps
- Milestone celebrations

### Sample Captions
- "We couldn't have said it better ourselves 💬 Thank you @username"
- "3 months in, 3,700 of you. Merci infiniment 🙏"
- "Last night's private event was pure magic ✨"
- "Proud to partner with @localfarm for the freshest produce"
- "Your moments at Epictete make everything worth it"

### Engagement Strategy
- Always credit original creators
- Ask permission before reposting
- Engage with comments
- Feature diverse guests
- Celebrate community milestones

## Content Do's and Don'ts

### DO ✅
- Maintain visual consistency
- Tell stories, not just show
- Include branded elements
- Use natural, warm lighting
- Show human moments
- Vary content formats
- Engage with comments
- Use consistent hashtags

### DON'T ❌
- Post low-quality images
- Use heavy filters that distort colors
- Over-post (quality > quantity)
- Ignore comments
- Be overly promotional
- Post without strategy
- Forget the philosophy angle
- Neglect Stories engagement
