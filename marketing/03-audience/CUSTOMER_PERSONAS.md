## Overview

These personas represent Epictete's primary target audience segments. Use them to guide marketing messaging, content creation, and service decisions.

### Restaurant Context
- **Cuisine:** Contemporary Mediterranean (Italian-focused)
- **Price Point:** 45-170 DHS per dish (upper-mid range, ~300-600 MAD/person)
- **Location:** Bouskoura Sud (27184) - upscale suburb, 20-30 min from Casablanca center
- **Key Differentiators:** Own organic farm, Stoic philosophy, wood-fired pizza, Instagram-worthy design

### Local Market Intelligence (Priority Zone)

**Bouskoura Industrial Zone (5-15 min drive)**
- **41 Major Companies** | **12,467+ Employees** | **€5B+ market value**
- **Top Employers:** STMicroelectronics (4,000), Varun Beverages (2,000), Oulmès (1,500), Sothema (500)
- **Key Sectors:** Semiconductors, Pharma, Aerospace, Beverages, Fintech
- **10 Multinational HQs:** STMicroelectronics, Varun/PepsiCo, Brenntag, Leoni, Alcoa, Eczacibasi, etc.

**Residential Zones (High Income)**
- **Quartier Prestigia:** Upscale gated community, professionals & executives
- **Bouskoura Golf City:** Premium residences, expats, business owners
- **Ville Verte:** Modern family homes, upper-middle class

### Targeting Strategy: Bouskoura-First

| Priority | Zone | Distance | Audience Type |
|----------|------|----------|---------------|
| 🥇 **Primary** | Bouskoura + Prestigia | 0-15 min | Local residents + Industrial employees |
| 🥈 **Secondary** | Nouaceur + Dar Bouazza | 15-25 min | Nearby affluent suburbs |
| 🥉 **Tertiary** | Casablanca (Anfa, Racine) | 25-40 min | City center high-income |

### Total Addressable Audience
- **Local Priority (Bouskoura radius):** ~50K-80K people (high conversion potential)
- **Extended Reach (Grand Casablanca):** 2.1M - 2.5M people (brand awareness)
