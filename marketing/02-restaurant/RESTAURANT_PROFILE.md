## 2. Concept & Philosophy

### Restaurant Concept
Epictete is a contemporary Mediterranean restaurant that marries Stoic philosophy with culinary excellence. Named after the Greek philosopher Epictetus (Épictète in French), the restaurant invites guests to practice mindful dining — savoring each moment and making intentional choices.

### Philosophy Statement
> "Savoure chaque instant, maîtrise chaque choix"  
> (Savor every moment, master every choice)

This philosophy manifests in:
- **Mindful Dining:** Encouraging guests to be present
- **Intentional Sourcing:** Organic ingredients from own farm
- **Considered Design:** Every detail serves the experience
- **Quality over Quantity:** Focused menu, perfected dishes

### Unique Selling Propositions (USPs)

have 1. **Farm-to-Table Fresh** — Organic vegetables and herbs from own farm
2. **Wood-Fired Excellence** — Authentic Neapolitan-style pizza oven
3. **Stoic Philosophy** — Unique conceptual framework for dining
4. **Mediterranean Fusion** — Italian techniques with Moroccan hospitality
5. **Artisanal Everything** — Handmade pastas, house sauces, fresh bread
6. **Design-Forward Space** — Instagram-worthy Art Deco Mediterranean interior

## 4. Atmosphere & Design

### Design Philosophy
> "Un lieu pensé pour l'élégance et le confort. Chaque ligne de lumière, chaque assise, chaque table raconte une histoire de raffinement."
> (A place designed for elegance and comfort. Each line of light, each seat, each table tells a story of refinement.)

**Theme:** Modern Mediterranean with Art Deco influences  
**Mood:** Warm, sophisticated, intimate yet open  
**Concept:** "Le marbre, le bois, la douceur des lumières et la précision des détails forment un écrin où le temps ralentit" — a contemporary setting where time slows down
