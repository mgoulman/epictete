## 1. Brand Identity Summary

### The Name
**Epictete** (Épictète in French) is named after **Epictetus**, the Stoic philosopher (c. 50-135 AD) who taught that happiness comes from mindful living and intentional choices.

### Core Philosophy
> "Savoure chaque instant, maîtrise chaque choix"  
> (Savor every moment, master every choice)

This translates Stoic principles into dining:
- **Be present** — Savor each bite
- **Make intentional choices** — Quality over quantity
- **Find joy in simplicity** — Honest, well-prepared food

### Brand Personality
- **Sophisticated** but not pretentious
- **Warm** and genuinely hospitable
- **Mindful** and intentional
- **Authentic** and transparent
- **Mediterranean** — relaxed elegance

## 2. Restaurant Overview

### Concept
Epictete is a contemporary Italian/Mediterranean restaurant combining:
- **Stoic philosophy** as conceptual framework
- **Organic, farm-fresh ingredients** from own farm
- **Wood-fired cooking** (traditional Neapolitan pizza oven)
- **Artisanal preparation** (handmade pastas, fresh sauces)
- **Design-forward space** (Art Deco Mediterranean interior)

### What Makes It Unique
1. Only restaurant with philosophical foundation (Stoic)
2. Own organic farm ("Bio de notre ferme") — **produces olives & olive oil as separate retail project**
3. Traditional wood-fired pizza oven
4. Instagram-worthy interior design
5. Bouskoura location (growing upscale suburb)
6. Vertical integration — from olive grove to table

### Service Style
- Table service
- Open kitchen concept
- Continuous service (service continu)
- Gastronomic cuisine with signature cocktails/mocktails
