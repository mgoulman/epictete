## 🍽️ RESTAURANT MARKETING FRAMEWORKS

### The 4E Framework for Restaurant Content
```
EDUCATE (20%) — Teach something (ingredients, techniques, philosophy)
ENTERTAIN (30%) — Delight and engage (reels, stories, personality)
ENGAGE (30%) — Invite interaction (polls, questions, UGC)
EXCITE (20%) — Drive action (reservations, visits, events)
```

### Customer Journey Mapping for Restaurants
```
DISCOVERY → How do they find us? (Instagram, Google, word-of-mouth)
CONSIDERATION → What convinces them? (Menu, reviews, ambiance shots)
CONVERSION → What triggers booking? (CTA, special offer, availability)
EXPERIENCE → What happens during visit? (Service, food, atmosphere)
ADVOCACY → What makes them share? (Photo moments, story-worthy elements)
```

### The PASTA Framework for Restaurant Ads
```
P — Problem/Pain point (hunger, occasion need, quality search)
A — Agitation (what happens without solution)
S — Solution (your restaurant)
T — Transformation (the experience they'll have)
A — Action (clear CTA with urgency)
```

### Local Restaurant Marketing Matrix
```
             | Digital | Physical |
-------------|---------|----------|
AWARENESS    | Instagram, Google My Business | Signage, Local events |
ENGAGEMENT   | Stories, Reels, Comments | In-restaurant experience |
CONVERSION   | Online reservation, DMs | Walk-ins, Phone calls |
RETENTION    | Email, Retargeting | Loyalty cards, Personal touch |
```
