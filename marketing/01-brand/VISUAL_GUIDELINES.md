## 1. Photography Direction

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Mood:** Warm, inviting, sophisticated
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred; warm artificial when needed
- **Color Temperature:** Warm tones (3000K-4000K feel)
- **Style:** Editorial meets lifestyle

## 2. Food Photography

### Composition Rules

**Angles:**
| Angle | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **Overhead (90°)** | Salads, pizzas, table spreads, flat-lay compositions |
| **Hero (45°)** | Plated dishes, desserts, layered items |
| **Eye Level (0°)** | Drinks, stacked items, dishes with height |
| **Detail (Macro)** | Textures, ingredients, toppings close-ups |

**Rule of Thirds:**
- Place hero dish at intersection points
- Leave breathing room for text overlay when needed

### Styling Requirements

**Always Include:**
- Branded plate (cream with navy rim)
- At least one branded element (placemat, napkin, cutlery holder)
- Natural garnish (microgreens, herbs, edible flowers)

**Props Palette:**
- Navy leather placemats
- Beige/cream leather placemats
- Gold-embroidered napkins
- Hexagonal leather coasters
- Olive wood serving boards
- Marble surface
- Fresh lemons (whole and sliced)
- Olive oil bottle
- Fresh herbs in frame
- Lemon water glasses

### Action Shots (Hero Content)

**Signature Techniques:**
1. **Oil Drizzle** — Pouring olive oil from height onto salads
2. **Sauce Pour** — Caramel or cream sauce being poured
3. **Powder Dust** — Cocoa, powdered sugar falling on desserts
4. **Cheese Grate** — Parmesan being grated over pasta
5. **Steam Rise** — Fresh-from-oven dishes with visible steam
6. **Fork Lift** — Lifting pasta or salad with fork mid-bite

### Color Balance

**Warm Foods:** Emphasize golden tones (grilled meats, pizzas, pastas)
**Fresh Foods:** Vibrant greens and reds (salads, vegetables)
**Desserts:** Rich browns and creams with berry accents

## 3. Interior Photography

### Key Areas to Capture

| Area | Focus Points |
|------|--------------|
| **Dining Room** | Velvet banquettes, natural light, table settings |
| **Feature Wall** | Bronze É monogram with backlighting |
| **Host Stand** | Art Deco wood details, brass accents |
| **Pizza Oven** | Flames, action, warmth |
| **Windows** | Natural light streaming in, gold frames |
| **Details** | Globe lights, greenery, marble surfaces |

### Time of Day

**Best Shooting Times:**
- **11am-2pm:** Natural light flooding through windows
- **Golden Hour (6-7pm):** Warm glow, romantic atmosphere
- **Evening Service:** Ambient lighting, candles, mood

### Composition Guidelines

- Capture depth with layered elements
- Include greenery in frame when possible
- Show scale with human elements (hands, partial figures)
- Highlight texture contrasts (velvet vs marble vs brass)

## 4. Social Media Formats

### Instagram Feed

**Square (1:1) — 1080x1080px**
- Classic food shots
- Detail shots
- Quote graphics

**Portrait (4:5) — 1080x1350px**
- Hero food shots
- Interior wide shots
- Optimal for engagement

### Instagram Stories/Reels

**Vertical (9:16) — 1080x1920px**
- Behind-the-scenes
- Action videos
- Daily specials
- Boomerangs (pouring, sprinkling)

### Video Guidelines

**Food Videos:**
- 5-15 seconds for Reels
- Smooth movements (tripod or gimbal)
- Include plating finish or action moment
- Natural sound or subtle music

**Atmosphere Videos:**
- Panning shots of interior
- Time-lapse of service
- Focus pulls between elements

## 5. Graphic Design Templates

### Social Post Templates

**Quote Cards:**
- Background: Cream (#E8E0D0) or Navy (#1B2B4D)
- Typography: Playfair Display
- Accent: Laurel wreath or É monogram
- Format: Square or 4:5

**Menu Highlights:**
- Clean white background
- Dish photo with soft shadow
- Olive green or gold text
- Price in smaller text

**Event Announcements:**
- Feature photography background
- Semi-transparent overlay (navy at 80%)
- Gold accent text for key info
- Laurel wreath decoration

### Print Materials

**Business Cards:**
- Front: Logo on cream
- Back: Contact info, navy text
- Paper: Thick matte with gold foil accent

**Menu:**
- Cover: Olive green leather
- Interior: Cream paper, olive green text
- Section dividers: Laurel wreath illustrations

## 6. Video & Motion

### Transitions
- Smooth fades
- Gentle zooms
- Avoid harsh cuts or trendy transitions

### Text Animation
- Elegant fade-in
- Subtle tracking animation
- Gold shimmer for special announcements

### Music/Audio Style
- Acoustic, Mediterranean-inspired
- Soft jazz
- Ambient restaurant sounds
- No aggressive or electronic music

## 7. User-Generated Content Guidelines

### Repost Criteria
✅ Good lighting and composition
✅ Branded elements visible
✅ Positive sentiment
✅ High-resolution image

### Editing UGC
- Apply consistent color grade
- Add subtle brand watermark
- Credit original creator

### Encouraging UGC
- Table tents with Instagram handle
- Photogenic moments designed into experience
- Staff trained to offer photos

## 8. Seasonal Adaptations

### Spring/Summer
- Brighter, lighter imagery
- Outdoor terrace (if applicable)
- Fresh salads, citrus focus
- More natural light

### Fall/Winter
- Warmer, moodier tones
- Cozy interior shots
- Comfort food focus
- Candlelight, evening ambiance

### Ramadan/Holidays
- Respectful, inclusive imagery
- Family-style platters
- Special menu highlights
- Warm, celebratory mood

## 9. Technical Specifications

### Image Delivery

| Use Case | Format | Resolution | Color Space |
|----------|--------|------------|-------------|
| Web | JPEG/WebP | 72 DPI | sRGB |
| Print | TIFF/PDF | 300 DPI | CMYK |
| Social | JPEG/PNG | 72 DPI | sRGB |
| Archive | RAW + JPEG | Full res | Original |

### File Naming
```
EPICTETE_[category]_[description]_[date]_[version].[format]

Examples:
EPICTETE_food_margherita-pizza_20260115_v1.jpg
EPICTETE_interior_dining-room_20260115_v1.jpg
EPICTETE_action_oil-pour-salad_20260115_v1.mp4
```

### Color Correction Settings

**Lightroom/Camera Raw Baseline:**
- Temperature: +5-10 (warmer)
- Tint: +5 (slightly magenta)
- Highlights: -15
- Shadows: +20
- Whites: +10
- Blacks: -10
- Vibrance: +15
- Saturation: +5
