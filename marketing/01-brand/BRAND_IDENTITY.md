# Epictete - Brand Identity Guide

> **Last Updated:** January 2026  
> **Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Brand Overview

### Brand Name
- **Full Name:** Epictete le Restaurant
- **Short Name:** Epictete
- **Pronunciation:** eh-pick-TET (French pronunciation)
- **Origin:** Named after Epictetus (Épictète in French), the Stoic philosopher — reflecting wisdom, mindfulness, and the art of savoring life

### Brand Philosophy
*"Savoure chaque instant, maîtrise chaque choix"*  
(Savor every moment, master every choice)

This philosophy draws from Stoic principles — being present, making conscious choices, and finding excellence in simplicity.

---

## 2. Logo & Monogram

### Primary Logo
- **Style:** Elegant serif typography with decorative flourishes
- **Text:** "Épictete" with stylized capital É
- **Character:** The É features an artistic swirl/flourish that becomes a distinctive brand element

### Monogram
- **Design:** Stylized "É" enclosed in a laurel wreath
- **Usage:** Plates, napkins, coasters, smaller applications
- **Symbolism:** Laurel wreath represents excellence, victory, and Mediterranean heritage

### Icon Mark
- **Design:** Large sculptural "É" in bronze/gold metal
- **Application:** Feature wall installation, backlit on stone/marble backdrop
- **Dimensions:** Approximately 1 meter height for main installation

## 4. Typography

### Primary Typeface (Headings)
- **Family:** Playfair Display (or similar elegant serif)
- **Weights:** Regular, Bold
- **Usage:** Logo, headlines, menu titles, signage
- **Character:** Classic, sophisticated, timeless

### Secondary Typeface (Body)
- **Family:** Inter (or similar clean sans-serif)
- **Weights:** Regular, Medium, SemiBold
- **Usage:** Body text, descriptions, digital applications
- **Character:** Modern, legible, approachable

### Logo Typography
- **Style:** Custom serif with decorative swash on É
- **Characteristics:** 
  - Elegant curves
  - High contrast between thick and thin strokes
  - Distinctive flourishes on capital letters

## 6. Interior Design Elements

### Design Philosophy
> "Un lieu pensé pour l'élégance et le confort. Chaque ligne de lumière, chaque assise, chaque table raconte une histoire de raffinement."

**Theme:** Modern Mediterranean with Art Deco influences  
**Concept:** "Le marbre, le bois, la douceur des lumières et la précision des détails forment un écrin où le temps ralentit"

### Exterior / Facade
| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Main Signage** | Neon "Epictete" in gold cursive with decorative flourishes |
| **Secondary Sign** | "Le Restaurant" below main sign |
| **Entrance** | Large arched glass doors, black frames |
| **Display** | Vitrine visible from outside |
| **Seasonal** | Fairy lights for festive occasions |

### Key Interior Features
| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Banquettes** | Olive green velvet, continuous booth-style along walls |
| **Chairs** | Rattan/cane with curved backs, warm wood frames |
| **Tables** | White/cream marble tops, various sizes |
| **Flooring** | Dark terrazzo with aggregate |
| **Lighting** | Globe pendant lights on gold/brass fixtures |
| **Windows** | Tall with gold frames, arched architectural details |
| **Feature Wall** | Large bronze É on illuminated marble/stone backdrop |
| **Mirrors** | Mirrored/glass panels adding depth |
| **Greenery** | Monstera, palm trees, olive trees, potted plants |
| **Planters** | Cylindrical white marble containers |
| **Pizza Oven** | Traditional wood-fired brick, visible open kitchen |

### Pizza Oven
- **Type:** Traditional wood-fired brick oven
- **Visibility:** Open kitchen concept, visible flame
- **Significance:** Authentic Neapolitan-style pizza preparation

## 7. Photography Style

### Food Photography
- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred, warm and moody for evening shots
- **Angles:** Overhead flat-lay and 45-degree hero shots
- **Props:** Always include branded elements (placemats, napkins, plates)
- **Style:** Editorial, appetizing, highlighting textures and colors

### Signature Techniques
- **Action Shots:** Drizzling oil, sprinkling powdered sugar, pouring sauces
- **Steam/Smoke:** Capture freshness and warmth
- **Hands in Frame:** Human element for lifestyle feel

### Interior Photography
- **Focus:** Natural light through windows, warm ambient lighting
- **Highlight:** Greenery, textures (velvet, marble, brass)
- **Mood:** Inviting, aspirational, sophisticated

## 8. Brand Values

1. **Excellence (Ἀρετή)** — Pursuit of quality in every detail
2. **Mindfulness** — Being present, savoring each moment
3. **Authenticity** — Farm-to-table, organic ingredients
4. **Craftsmanship** — Traditional techniques, artisanal approach
5. **Hospitality** — Warm, attentive, memorable service

---

## 9. Brand Don'ts

- ❌ Do not use the logo in unapproved colors
- ❌ Do not stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Do not use low-resolution images
- ❌ Do not pair with overly modern/tech aesthetics
- ❌ Do not use cold/clinical color temperatures
- ❌ Do not use casual or playful typography
- ❌ Do not photograph food without branded elements visible
