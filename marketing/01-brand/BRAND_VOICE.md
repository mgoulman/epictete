## 1. Brand Voice Overview

### Core Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Sophisticated** | Refined without being pretentious | "An evening crafted for discerning palates" |
| **Warm** | Inviting and genuine hospitality | "Welcome to your table" |
| **Mindful** | Intentional, present, considered | "Each dish tells a story of patience and passion" |
| **Authentic** | Honest, transparent, real | "From our farm to your plate" |
| **Mediterranean** | Relaxed elegance, joie de vivre | "Savor the moment, taste the Mediterranean" |

## 3. Tone Guidelines

### General Tone
- **DO:** Be warm, inviting, cultured, aspirational
- **DON'T:** Be cold, salesy, overly casual, or pretentious

### Tone by Channel

| Channel | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Instagram** | Warm, visual, inviting | "Sunday afternoons were made for this 🌿" |
| **Website** | Elegant, informative, welcoming | "Discover a dining experience where every detail matters" |
| **Reservations** | Professional, accommodating | "We look forward to welcoming you" |
| **In-Restaurant** | Personal, attentive, genuine | "May I recommend our chef's special this evening?" |
| **Email** | Warm, personal, appreciative | "Thank you for choosing to dine with us" |

## 4. Language & Vocabulary

### Preferred Words

| Category | Use These | Avoid These |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Food** | Crafted, prepared, curated, seasonal | Made, cooked, created |
| **Experience** | Journey, moment, discovery, ritual | Event, time, occasion |
| **Quality** | Artisanal, authentic, organic, fresh | Best, premium, luxury |
| **Service** | Attentive, thoughtful, warm | Fast, efficient, quick |
| **Atmosphere** | Intimate, refined, inviting | Fancy, exclusive, elite |

## 7. Writing Style Rules

### General Rules
1. **Sentence case** for headings (not TITLE CASE)
2. **Oxford comma** for lists
3. **Active voice** preferred
4. **Short paragraphs** (2-3 sentences max)
5. **Em dashes** for emphasis — like this

### Numbers
- Spell out one through ten
- Use numerals for 11+
- Always use numerals for prices, times, dates

### Punctuation
- Use ellipses sparingly...
- Exclamation points: maximum one per post
- Emojis: 1-2 per social post, use sparingly

### Emoji Guidelines

**Approved Emojis:**
- 🌿 (freshness, organic)
- ✨ (special moments)
- 🔥 (wood-fired, passion)
- 🍕 (pizza content)
- 🏛️ (philosophy, heritage)
- 🕰️ (timelessness)

**Avoid:**
- 😂🤣 (too casual)
- 💯🙌 (too trendy)
- Multiple fire emojis 🔥🔥🔥

## 9. Messaging Don'ts

- ❌ Never use "cheap," "deal," "discount" language
- ❌ Avoid aggressive sales tactics
- ❌ Don't overpromise or use superlatives excessively
- ❌ Never criticize competitors
- ❌ Avoid slang or overly trendy language
- ❌ Don't use all caps for emphasis
- ❌ Never post without proofreading
- ❌ Avoid controversial topics unrelated to food
